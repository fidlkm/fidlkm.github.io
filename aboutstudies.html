My Studies & Educational Background

Overview

I currently study at the University of Florida in Gainesville, Florida. I'm a 4th year Computer Engineering student on track to graduate in the spring of 2016. My main academic interests include digital hardware design, cyber security, and robotics. Prior to attending UF, I studied at Florida State College at Jacksonville, where I earned my Associate of Arts degree and graduated with high honors.

Extra-Curriculars

In addition to my studies, I try to stay actively involved with a number of student groups at the university. In the past, I have been a part of the Gator Robotics Outreach Team, which brings fun, engaging lessons in physics and engineering to local schools and libraries in order to encourage children to pursue engineering disciplines. I have since held similar positions in both the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers(IEEE) and the Eta Kappa Nu Honor Society. I also currently hold a chair position with the UF IEEE chapter, and am in charge of contacting recruiters from various tech/engineering companies around the country in order to schedule IEEE-hosted on-campus recruiting events.
