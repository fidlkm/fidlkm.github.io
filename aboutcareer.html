Career Background

Being that I'm still a college undergrad, I don't exactly have a multitude of industry experience. I completed my first internship this summer. I had the awesome opportunity to work as a software engineering intern for Lockheed Martin at their Missiles and Fire Control Facility in Orlando, FL. While there, I was assigned to work on an Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconaissance (ISR) system currently being developed. I designed software related to Host platform communication for command and control purposes, Global Positioning System (GPS) communications software, and implementing logic to utilize fallback application boot image in cases where current boot application image encounters issues at system power up.
